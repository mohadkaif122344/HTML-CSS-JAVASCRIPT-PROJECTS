KINGFISHERS
Kingfishers are a family, the Alcedinidae, of small to medium-sized, brightly colored birds in the order Coraciiformes. They have a cosmopolitan distribution, with most species found in the tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Oceania, but also can be seen in Europe.

RAINBOW LORIKEET
The Rainbow Lorikeet is a vibrant, nectar-feeding parrot native to Australia. It has striking rainbow-colored plumage and is known for its playful, social behavior.
SLIDER 4
BUTTERFLY
A butterfly is a colorful, delicate insect with large, patterned wings. It undergoes metamorphosis from caterpillar to adult. Butterflies pollinate flowers, aiding ecosystems. They symbolize transformation and beauty, often seen fluttering gracefully in gardens, meadows, and sunny natural habitats.
SLIDER 5
OWL
Owls are nocturnal birds known for silent flight, sharp talons, and excellent night vision. They have large eyes, a rotating head, and a flat face. Owls hunt small animals and symbolize wisdom, mystery, and intuition in many cultures worldwide.
SLIDER 6
EAGLE
Eagles are powerful birds of prey known for their sharp vision, strong talons, and majestic flight. They soar high with broad wings, hunting small animals. Found worldwide, eagles symbolize freedom, courage, and dominance in many cultures and mythologies..
SLIDER 7
RED FOX
The red fox is a clever, adaptable mammal with reddish fur, a bushy tail, and sharp senses, commonly found in forests, grasslands, and cities..
SLIDER 8
HUMMINGBIRD
Hummingbirds are tiny, colorful birds known for their rapid wing beats and ability to hover mid-air. They feed on nectar, using long beaks and tongues. Agile fliers, they play a key role in pollination and thrive in vibrant habitats.
SLIDER 9
ZEBRA
Zebras are herbivorous mammals known for their distinctive black-and-white striped coats. Native to Africa, they live in herds and graze on grasses. Their stripes help with camouflage and predator confusion. Zebras are social, alert, and related to horses and donkeys.
SLIDER 10
BEAR
Bears are large, powerful mammals with thick fur, sharp claws, and strong limbs. Found in forests, mountains, and arctic regions, they are omnivores. Some hibernate in winter. Bears symbolize strength, resilience, and are vital to ecosystem balance.
SLIDER 11
PARROT
Parrots are colorful, intelligent birds known for mimicking sounds. They have curved beaks, strong feet, and live in tropical regions, often forming strong social bonds..
